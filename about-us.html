About us

SHALATCO PRIVATE LIMITED was established in 1990.The main activity of SHALATCO is Manufacturing and Export of Surgical, Dental and Veterinary Instruments and Scissors of all sorts. We have the Equipment and the know how to manufacture the quality Instruments. We are pioneers in giving a new dimension to the Veterinary Instruments and tools. The traditional raw material used in producing these instruments was Carbon Steel chrome plated but we have set a new trend of introducing ASTM Standard Stainless Steel as a substitute to the old material.
